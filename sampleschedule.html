Sample Schedule

This is a tentative pathway for a student aspiring to major in Civil Engineering.

First Year

Fall Semester

  • MATH 0220: Analytic Geometry and Calculus 1
  • CHEM 0960: General Chem for Engineers 1
  • PHYS 0174: Basic Physics, Science and Engineering 1 (Integrated)
  • ENGR 0011: Intro to Engineering Analysis
  • ENGR 0081: Freshman Engineering Seminar 1
  • ENGCMP 0210: Seminar in Composition: Engineering

Spring Semester

  • MATH 0230: Analytic Geometry and Calculus 2
  • CHEM 0970: Analytic Geometry and Calculus 2
  • PHYS 0175: Basic Physics, Science and Engineering 2 (Integrated)
  • ENGR 0012: Intro to Engineering Computing
  • ENGR 0082: Freshman Engineering Seminar 2
  • ENGCMP 0412: Engineering Communication in a Professional Context

Second Year

Fall Semester

  • MATH 0240: Analytic Geometry and Calculus 3
  • ENGR 0131: Statics for Civil and Environmental Engineers
  • IE 1040: Engineering Economic Analysis
  • CEE 1503: Intro to Environmental Engineering
  • Science Elective
  • CEE 1085: Departmental Seminar

Spring Semester

  • MATH 0290: Differential Equations
  • ENGR 0141: MECHC of MATLS CVL & ENV ENGR
  • CEE 0109: Computer Methods in Civil Engrg 1
  • CEE 1105: Materials of Construction
  • ECON 0100: Intro Microeconomic Theory
  • Science Elective
  • CEE 1085: Departmental Seminar

Third Year

Fall Semester

  • CEE 1330: Intro to Structural Analysis
  • CEE 1402: Fluid Machanics
  • CEE 1811: Principles of Soil Mechanics
  • ENGR 0151: Dynamics CIVIL & ENVRN ENGNRS
  • Social Science Elective
  • CEE 1085: Departmental Seminar

Spring Semester

  • CEE 1200: Construction Management
  • CEE 1609: Life Cycle Assessment Methods Tools
  • CEE 1412: Introduction to Hydrology
  • CEE 1703: Transportation Engineering
  • CEE Design Elective
  • CEE 1085: Departmental Seminar

Fourth Year

Fall Semester

  • CEE: Elective
  • CEE: Elective
  • CE: Design Elective
  • Engineering Elective
  • Humanities/Social Science Elective
  • CEE 1085: Departmental Seminar

Spring Semester

  • Humanities/Social Science Elective
  • CEE 1333: Structural Design Project
  • CEE 1533: ENVRL ENGR Design Project
  • CEE Elective
  • CEE Elective
  • CEE Elective
